## DESCRIPTION

- Assessment 1A Problem Solving Exercise
- DUE DATE: Sunday, Week 5 @ 23:59 (Melbourne time)
- TOPICS ASSESSED: (weeks 1-5)
  - Functions,
  - dicts,
  - more repetition,
  - user input,
  - string formats,
  - Lambda expressions
- 5% of total grade for COSC2531

In this exercise you will create a program which calculates the price for a specific purchase based
on a product, a customer and a quantity. Each product has a unit price and each customer has a
customer discount. When a product is purchased the unit price, the quantity ordered, and the
customer discount are used to computer the total price of the purchase. The products, prices and
other data that the program uses are up to you.
